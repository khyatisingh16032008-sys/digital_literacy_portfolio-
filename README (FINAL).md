## 📌 Task Summaries

### ✅ Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Created a one-page visual infographic using **Canva** covering five key topics: what digital literacy is, safe internet practices, professional online presence, useful digital tools for students, and email etiquette. The infographic is designed to help batchmates quickly understand the importance of being digitally literate.

### ✅ Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Set up professional profiles on **GitHub**, **LinkedIn**, and **Kaggle** to build a foundational digital presence for academic and career purposes. Each platform serves a distinct role — GitHub for code projects, LinkedIn for professional networking, and Kaggle for data science practice.

### ✅ Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Completed a beginner coding challenge on **HackerRank** (Python domain) and created a 5-question **Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz** using Google Forms, linked to a Google Sheet for automatic response tracking.

> 🔗 **Google Form Link:** https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSffKbXtxqarTNm8gHK_gldaKyQwx3qgJgBMzMTQFTieMv6Efg/viewform?usp=header

### ✅ Task 4 – Professional Email & Etiquette
Drafted two professional emails — one requesting an assignment extension from a professor, and one expressing interest in a summer internship. Also created a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist for responsible digital communication as a college student.

### ✅ Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Researched and wrote a detailed case study on **UPI/Online Payment Fraud**, describing how the scam works step-by-step, who it targets, and its consequences. Created a 10-point prevention checklist specifically for college students in India, including the National Cyber Crime Helpline **1930** and portal **cybercrime.gov.in**.
